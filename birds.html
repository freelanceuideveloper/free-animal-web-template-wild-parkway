Birds

Explore the fascinating world of feathered creatures, masters of the sky with incredible diversity and adaptations.

About Birds

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by feathers, beaked jaws, the laying of hard-shelled eggs, and a lightweight but strong skeleton. They have evolved to occupy nearly every habitat on Earth.

Bald Eagle

The national bird of the United States, known for its impressive wingspan and keen eyesight.

North America Stable
Peacock

Famous for their spectacular tail displays, peacocks are among the most beautiful birds in the world.

Asia Stable
Hummingbird

Tiny birds that can hover in mid-air and fly backwards, with the fastest wingbeat of any bird.

Americas Stable
Penguin

Flightless birds perfectly adapted for life in water, excellent swimmers and divers.

Antarctica Various
Owl

Silent nocturnal hunters with exceptional hearing and night vision capabilities.

Worldwide Stable
Parrot

Highly intelligent birds known for their ability to mimic human speech and use tools.

Tropics Vulnerable
