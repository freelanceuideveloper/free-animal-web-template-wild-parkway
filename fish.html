Fish

Dive into the underwater world of fish, from tiny tropical species to massive ocean giants.

About Fish

Fish are aquatic vertebrates with gills, fins, and typically scales. They inhabit waters from the deepest oceans to mountain streams, showing incredible diversity in size, shape, and behavior.

Great White Shark

Apex predator of the oceans, perfectly evolved for hunting with incredible speed and power.

Global Oceans Vulnerable
Clownfish

Colorful reef fish that live in symbiosis with sea anemones, protected by their venomous tentacles.

Coral Reefs Stable
Anglerfish

Deep-sea predators that use bioluminescent lures to attract prey in the darkness of the ocean depths.

Deep Ocean Stable
Electric Eel

Capable of generating powerful electric shocks up to 600 volts for hunting and self-defense.

South America Stable
Tuna

Fast-swimming migratory fish prized for their meat, capable of swimming at speeds up to 45 mph.

Global Oceans Overfished
Seahorse

Unique fish where males carry and give birth to young, with prehensile tails and independently moving eyes.

Coastal Waters Vulnerable

Fascinating Fish Facts

Over 34,000 fish species exist worldwide

Fish have been on Earth for over 500 million years

Some fish can change gender during their lifetime

The oldest known fish lived to 205 years old
